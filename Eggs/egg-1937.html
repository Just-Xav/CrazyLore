SCP-1937 (Jewish egg)

Object Class: Euclid

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-1937 is to be maintained in an airtight capsule, the capsule should rest in a large body of water which MUST include a stone statue of emma frost from marvel rivals. Subject should be fed 'The Decaying Egg' At LEAST once a day, failure to do so will result in instant combustion. Failure to keep SCP-1937 in these conditions will result in him jacking off immediatley.

Description:

SCP-1937 (Jewish egg) is a unicellular being that solely exists to be in pain. He is a result of not watching the minecraft movie and explains his constant desire to jack off or combust. Despite being held in an airtight capsule, SCP-1937 has the capabilities to release gas, Blackus mouldus leslieus gaseous (a subproduct of Blackus mouldus leslieus), which has properties which allows it to create combustion. His behaviour is simlar to the Asian egg, but on a magnitude much greater and much more harsh living conditions.
